## Introduction

Two stocks that have recently attracted significant market attention are:

- Ather Energy Ltd  
- Anand Rathi Wealth Ltd  

Both companies have **broken out of prolonged consolidation phases** and are now trading near or at their **all-time highs (ATH)**. Such price action often indicates strong momentum and improving investor sentiment.

---

## Understanding the Breakout

A breakout from a consolidation zone typically suggests:

- Fresh buying interest from institutions  
- Shift from accumulation to trending phase  
- Reduced supply at higher levels  

When a stock sustains above its previous range and moves into uncharted territory (All Time High), it often enters a **momentum-driven phase**, where trend continuation becomes more probable.
